How to use Sight Word Pop

Sight Word Pop helps early readers practice listening for sight words and matching them to colorful balloons.

Quick start

  1. Open the app. Your child will see sight-word balloons floating on the screen.
  2. Tap the green Start button. The app will say one sight word out loud.
  3. Tap the matching balloon. If the word is correct, the balloon pops and the app says another word.
  4. Try again after a buzz. If the wrong balloon is tapped, the app buzzes and the same word stays active.
  5. Pop every balloon. The round ends with a congratulations screen.

Tips for grown-ups

  • Turn the iPad volume up before starting.
  • Use the game in a quiet room so the spoken word is easy to hear.
  • Encourage your child to say the word after each balloon pops.
  • Use the “Say it again” button whenever the word needs to be repeated.

Troubleshooting

I cannot hear the word.

Check the iPad volume, make sure Silent Mode is off, and try the “Say it again” button. If you are using the website version, open it in Safari and tap Start before expecting speech.

The balloons are not moving.

The iPad may have Reduce Motion enabled. The game still works, but animations can be minimized by the device setting.

Can I add more words?

The current version uses a fixed starter list of sight words. More word sets can be added in a future update.

Does the app collect data?

No. Sight Word Pop does not require an account, does not show ads, and does not collect personal information.
