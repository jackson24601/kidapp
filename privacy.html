Privacy Policy

Simple games. No data collection.

Our kids' apps are simple learning games. They do not require accounts, do not show ads, and do not collect personal information.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

This privacy policy applies to Sight Word Pop and other simple kids' games published by us. These apps are designed for children and families to use without accounts, tracking, advertising, or data collection.

Information we collect

We do not collect, store, sell, rent, or share personal information from children, parents, or other users.

  • No names or email addresses are collected in the app.
  • No account or login is required.
  • No photos, contacts, location, or device identifiers are collected.
  • No gameplay history is sent to us.

Ads, analytics, and tracking

The apps do not include advertising, analytics tools, third-party tracking SDKs, or in-app purchases.

  • No behavioral advertising.
  • No third-party analytics.
  • No cross-app or cross-website tracking.
  • No sale or sharing of user data.

Children's privacy

Our apps are intended to be safe and simple for children. Because the apps do not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 or from any other user.

If you believe a child has sent personal information to us outside the app, please contact us so we can delete it.

How the apps work

Sight Word Pop runs on your device and uses device audio to speak words during the game. The app does not send those words, taps, sounds, or activity to us.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page with the new effective date. Any future app features that collect data would be clearly disclosed before release.
